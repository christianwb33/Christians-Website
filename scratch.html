The Pinnacle of Course Design

Having traveled to over 10 states to play competitively, I have developed a deep appreciation for course architecture. Here are four of the most legendary and challenging courses in the nation.

The Bucket List

  1. The Olympic Club
    • Location: San Francisco, California
    • Defining Feature: The Lake Course's reverse-camber fairways.
  2. Pebble Beach Golf Links
    • Location: Pebble Beach, California
    • Defining Feature: The iconic par-3 7th hole dropping toward the ocean.
  3. Augusta National Golf Club
    • Location: Augusta, Georgia
    • Defining Feature: Amen Corner and The Masters Tournament.
  4. Torrey Pines Golf Course
    • Location: La Jolla, California
    • Defining Feature: Rugged coastal cliffs and treacherous kikuyu rough.
